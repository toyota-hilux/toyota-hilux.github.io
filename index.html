About Me

About Me

I'm a 2nd Year Computational Sciencee student.

Hello, my name is Faaris and I am a second-year Computer Science student at UNIMAS. I have a deep passion for the IT field and believe that it represents the future of our world. Since high school, I have been interested in programming, and during my SPM project, I achieved the highest mark in my class by building a website with both front-end and back-end components. Although the project presented some challenges, I found the experience to be both challenging and rewarding. In the future, I aspire to start my own IT business, either as a solo venture or as a partner with another company. While I am aware of the financial potential of the field, I am equally motivated by my love for technology and my desire to continue learning and growing as a professional. I look forward to completing my studies in the next two years and honing my skills and knowledge in pursuit of my goals.
